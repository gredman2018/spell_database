manifestation_spell

this spell aims to focus your energy and manifest your desires. the kind of energy you put out into the cosmos will be returned to you accordingly. keep that in mind when you decide upon your manifestation.

items needed

  • bay leaf (included)
  • source of fire (included, but there are safer options readily available as well)
  • pen
  • non flammable bowl (metal, glass, etc.)

instructions

  1. focus your intentions. take a moment to determine what you wish to manifest.
  2. write your manifestation on your bay leaf.
  3. close your eyes while holding your leaf and visualize the energy in your body and the energy around you. hold your energy close, expand it and then cast it out into the universe. imagine your energy soaring out into the cosmos and reuniting with the energy that is present in all life.
  4. after a few moments, burn your leaf. if in an apartment building, do so near a window and over/in a non-flammable bowl. keep water on hand in case the fire accelerates.
